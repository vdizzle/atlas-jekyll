What we do

Web Applications

We develop complete web applications with data processing at its core. This includes building data pipelines, architecting RDBMS / NoSQL backends, creating RESTful APIs and rich single-page front-ends.

Custom Dashboards

We build customized web dashboards so that you know exactly whats happening in your business. Integrate all your disparate business data in one beautifully crafted view that fits equally well in a large TV screen or your mobile device.

Interactive Visualizations

Sometimes you need to go beyond the standard charts in business dashboards to tell compelling visual stories. We also create one-off stand-alone information visualizations for such cases.
